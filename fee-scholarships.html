Fee & Scholarships

Fee structure, scholarships and education loan guidance for applicants and continuing students.

Fees vary by programme, campus and year of entry. The live site publishes the current fee structure per programme; this rebuild reproduces the page layout rather than the fee tables.

Scholarships recognise academic merit, sport and performance, and financial need. Education loan guidance is provided for students financing study through a bank.

Support available

Merit scholarships

Awards based on qualifying examination performance and entrance test rank.

Need-based support

Assistance for students whose circumstances would otherwise prevent enrolment.

Sport and performing arts

Awards for students representing the university in sport or the performing arts.

Education loans

Guidance on documentation and bank processes for financing a programme.

Fee structure

Programme-by-programme fee detail, published per campus and academic year.

Payment

On the live site, fee payment runs through the university payment portal.
